Who is Eager Tom?

Eager Tom (née Thomas Mark) Eldridge is many things to many people. He grew up in Sheffield, England, where he spent his formative years, enjoying school, climbing in the Peak District and playing music. Aged 18 he moved to Bristol to study physics, got involved in the university's climbing and mountaineering society, partied hard and made friends for life. He spent his third year studying in Darmstadt, Germany, taking his high-school German to nearly fluent levels, before moving back to the UK to finish his undergraduate degree and begin postgraduate research in meteorology.

In 2021, motivated by a desire for new challenges and to keep up his German, he took a job in St Gallen, Switzerland. Although he sometimes chafes against certain cultural differences, he enjoys his life here and the opportunity it affords him to spend more time in the mountains, and has ultimately decided to stay, although his work is no longer in the same town.

Eager's professional skills lie somewhere in between scientist and software developer. He's a rational sort, and likes to do things rigorously, but he's not that interested in the minutiae of modern physics, preferring applied maths for modelling real-world scenarios.

Another thing that sets him apart from your stereotypical nerd is his breadth-not-depth approach. He has many interests, and a diverse array of friends. He also has a dry, quirky sense of humour and enjoys indulging himself in jokes which others might think have run their course.

What do you want to do with Eager Tom?

Recently, Eager's been taking his career more seriously, strengthening his professional skills, and working on a variety of personal software projects. Eager learnt to play classical music from an early age, and developed a real passion for listening to many kinds of music as he grew up. He's now interested in writing and producing his own music. Check it out!

See what he's been up to?

Eager likes to keep it interesting in his spare time. As well as the projects mentioned above, he stays active by cycling, climbing and running, he travels occasionally, and he participates in cultural events both at home and abroad.

Through listening to podcasts, reading books and simply chatting with friends, Eager likes to think he maintains a balanced yet nuanced perspective, with some genuinely different and interesting ideas thrown into the mix. Perhaps you'll agree?
